selected publications

  1. AAAI
    Hybrid phenology modeling for predicting temperature effects on tree dormancy
    Ron Bree , Diego Marcos , and Ioannis N Athanasiadis
    In Proceedings of the AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence , 2025
    Abs Bib Code

    We present a hybrid approach combining deep learning with phenological modeling to predict temperature effects on tree dormancy. Our method integrates domain knowledge from traditional phenology models with the flexibility of neural networks, demonstrating improved accuracy in predicting bud break timing under varying temperature conditions.

  2. ONE
    Transdisciplinary coordination is essential for advancing agricultural modeling with machine learning
    Lily-belle Sweet , Ioannis N Athanasiadis , Ron Bree , Andres Castellano , and 4 more authors
    One Earth, 2025
    Abs Bib

    This perspective paper discusses the importance of transdisciplinary coordination in advancing agricultural modeling through machine learning. We identify key challenges and opportunities in combining domain expertise with modern ML approaches.

  3. CVPR
    Neural prototype trees for interpretable fine-grained image recognition
    Meike Nauta , Ron Bree , and Christin Seifert
    In Proceedings of the IEEE/CVF conference on computer vision and pattern recognition , 2021
    Abs Bib PDF Code

    We propose Neural Prototype Trees (ProtoTrees), a novel interpretable deep learning architecture for fine-grained image recognition. Our model combines prototype learning with decision trees, enabling both accurate and interpretable predictions.
